ABOUT

The aim of the project is to provide Veterans with mental health issues art classes, giving them an alternate means of self-expression, fun, focus or just distraction. We believe that art is a vital tool for improving communication as well as a chance to create something of our own. We are hoping to expand the nature of the activities and our participants to wider members of the military community including families and active serving personnel.


OUR WORKWe currently run 6 classes a month in Las Vegas, split between the inpatient community at the VA Southern Nevada Healthcare system and the outpatient community at the VA Mental health recovery centre on W. Cheyenne Ave. These sessions typically last between 1-2 hours for anywhere up to 15 veterans per class.

GET INVOLVEDOur volunteers facilitate the art part of the class. The choice of activity is down to them, such as life drawing, landscapes, portraits, still life or whatever you can imagine! You do not need to be Picasso, just enthusiastic and flexible enough to adapt a plan based on the needs of the class, using the materials provided by the project. No mental health supervisory qualifications or experience are required. This aspect is covered by VA recreational therapists who work alongside us, they are great people and always willing to help with ideas and experience. If you are interested in volunteering to lead our classes or would like to help out in another capacity, please do not hesitate to reach out via our contact us section.
